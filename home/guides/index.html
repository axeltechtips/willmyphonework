How To Identify Your Device Model

Android

To find your model number on your Android device please see this link: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HxbqrWrrNtg

Apple

In order to identify your Apple device, please visit the following link: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ABdLR1qPZ5Y

Additional information:
iPhone: http://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201296
iPad: http://support.apple.com/en-ca/HT201471

BlackBerry

In order to identify your BlackBerry 10 device, please visit the following link: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WR5AUJiX5L8

For older BlackBerry OS devices, see the following: http://helpblog.blackberry.com/2013/04/identify-blackberry-model-and-software/

Nokia and Windows Phone

In order to identify your Windows and Nokia Lumia device, please visit the following link: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t1nV6Hkn7qQ

On old Nokia (non Windows Phone) devices, see the following: http://www.microsoft.com/en-ca/mobile/support/where-can-i-find-my-model-number/
