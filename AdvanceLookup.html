Advance Lookup

The advanced lookup section allows you to perform mobile device compatibility checks across multiple device brands, models, countries, and carriers. In addition, you can specify specific networks that must be compatible, such as 4G LTE.

Select the desired mobile device brand(s), model(s), and sub-model(s) (if applicable). Then choose the desired countries, and carrier(s). In the Network Compatibility drop down, choose the network(s) that must be compatible between the device(s) and carrier(s). Finally, there is a “show devices only with Full Compatibility” option. Choosing this will exclude devices that are partially compatible from the results.
